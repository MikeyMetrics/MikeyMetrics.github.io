Hamari mulakat WePlay ke uss voice room mein hui thi... jahan dher saare log the par mujhe sirf tumhari awaaz sunai de rahi thi. Aur woh **Jackaroo**! Yaad hai? Jab tum harne lagti ho toh kaise cheating karti ho aur phir masoomiyat se bolti ho "Main toh seekh rahi hoon". Bhai, tumse bada gamer koi nahi hai (cheating mein! 😂).

Lekin sach toh ye hai ki tumse baat karte karte kab tum meri sabse acchi dost se meri poori duniya ban gayi, pata hi nahi chala. Tumhari wo random baatein, wo gussa, aur phir pyar se manana—ye sab mere din ka best part hota hai.

Ye sitare jo piche chamak rahe hain? Inhe touch karke dekho, ye tumhari muskurahat ki tarah hain. Hamesha aise hi rehna, mere saath, meri favorite partner banke.
