For My Sally...

Sally, pata hai? Ranchi mein jab baarish hoti hai toh sab log ghar bhagte hain, par main terrace pe khada hoke tumhare baare mein sochta hoon (thoda filmy hai, par sach hai!).

Hamari mulakat WePlay ke uss voice room mein hui... wahan 50 log chilla rahe the par mujhe sirf tumhari awaaz sunai de rahi thi. Aur woh **Jackaroo**! Yaad hai? Jab tum harne lagti ho toh kaise cheating karti ho aur phir itni masoomiyat se bolti ho "Main toh seekh rahi hoon". Bhai, tumse bada gamer koi nahi hai (cheating mein! 😂).

Lekin sach toh ye hai ki tumse baat karte karte kab tum meri sabse acchi dost se meri duniya ban gayi, pata hi nahi chala. Tumhari wo random baatein, wo gussa, aur phir "Mikey" bol kar manana—ye sab mere din ka best part hota hai.

Log kehte hain online pyaar saccha nahi hota, unhe kya pata hamara **Voice Room Romance** kisi movie se kam nahi hai. Tum door ho, par is screen ke uss paar jo dil dhadakta hai, wo sirf tumhare liye hai.

Ye sitare jo screen pe dikh rahe hain na? Inhe touch karo... ye tumhari muskurahat ki tarah chamakne lagenge. Hamesha aise hi rehna, mere saath, meri "Cheating Partner" banke.

I Love You, Sally. ❤️
